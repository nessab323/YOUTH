Who We Are

About CAC Youth

We are a vibrant community of young believers passionate about faith, fellowship, and making a difference in our generation.

CAC Youth is a place where young people come together to worship, grow in their faith, and build lasting friendships. We believe that every young person has a unique purpose and calling, and we're here to help you discover yours.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time or you've been on this journey for years, there's a place for you here. Join us as we learn, serve, and grow together.
